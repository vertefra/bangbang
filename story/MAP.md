🗺️ World Map: The Dustfall Territories
1. The Hub: Dustfall Junction

A town built into the side of a red-rock canyon. The upper level is for the "respectable" folk, while the lower level (The Gully) is where the outlaws and workers congregate.

| Location | Description | Notable NPCs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Billy’s House | A modest, two-story wooden home on the edge of town. Smells of pine and flapjacks. | Martha (Mom) |
| The Silver Spur Saloon | The heart of town. Piano music plays constantly. A swinging door leads to the bar. | Barkeep Jack, Silas (if on Outlaw path) |
| First National Bank | A heavy stone building with iron bars. The symbol of order and corporate interest. | Mr. Sterling (Banker) |
| Sheriff’s Office & Jail | A small, sturdy building with two cells. Usually smells of old coffee and gunpowder. | Sheriff Miller, Deputy Hank |
| Sawbones Clinic | A clean but clinical room attached to the Doctor’s private residence. | Doc Sartori (The Medic) |
| The Emporium | A massive general store packed with crates. If it exists in the West, it’s on a shelf here. | Old Man Barnaby |

2. The Outskirts: The Scrublands

The immediate area surrounding the town. Open plains, cacti, and dusty trails.

| Location | Description | Notable NPCs |
| --- | --- | --- |
| The Hermit’s Shack | A ramshackle hut built against a cliff face, roughly 2 miles North of town. | The Headhunter (The "Edgy" Veteran) |
| The Old Mine Trail | A winding path leading into the mountains. Evidence of the "Corporate" expansion is everywhere. | Bandit Scouts, Calamity Kid (Rival) |
| The Watering Hole | A rare pond surrounded by greenery. A prime spot for finding Usable Skills (Herbs). | Wild Coyotes, Traveling Merchants |
